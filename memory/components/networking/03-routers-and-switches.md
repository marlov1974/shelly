# Routers and Switches

## Known devices

- Teltonika TSW030 DIN-mounted network switch.
- Teltonika/RUT-style routers, including RUT140 class devices, used or considered for technical Wi-Fi.
- Apple AirPort Express used as possible simple 2.4 GHz Wi-Fi for heat pump adapters.

## TSW030 fact

The Teltonika TSW030 has DIN mount on the back and occupies approximately two DIN modules in width.

## Technical Wi-Fi principle

For heat pumps and other legacy/IoT adapters, use stable 2.4 GHz settings rather than advanced roaming/band-steering features.
