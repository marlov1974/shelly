# Pumps

## Known pump families

- Grundfos MAGNA3 32-100 180
- Wilo/Yonos Pico 25/1-8-180
- Grundfos UPS/UPS32 legacy pumps

## Design principles

- Match pump to hydraulic resistance and required flow.
- Prefer smart pump internal modes for stable hydronic control.
- Avoid rapid external switching unless the pump and application tolerate it.
- Treat displayed flow with caution when resolution is coarse.

## Power observations

Small changes in pump head can materially increase pump power. Use measured power and delivered thermal effect together, not pump watts alone.
